the purpose of IT in modern business

IT has multiple purposes within modern business, ranging from the simple such as streamlining communication within the business as well as for both internal and external customers. This can be communication via E-Mail or as technology has evolved it could include live chat systems, online meeting tools, voice over internet protocol (VOIP) telephones and smartphones. Additional advantages of incorporating IT within a business include Inventory Management Systems which allow businesses to automatically track stock levels, and keep track of when new stock is required. There are also Data Management Systems which provide employees immediate access to files and documents which are stored on a businessâ€™ server. Management Information Systems (MIS) enable companies to track various elements related to the business; performance. Another aspect of IT which greatly benefits business are Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems, which allow the business to capture every interaction with a customer in order to refine their practises and provide a more enriching experience for the customer.
